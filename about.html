About Us

A trusted MEP engineering partner for Singapore's built environment.

Your trusted MEP engineering partner

NOVA M2 PTE LTD is a Singapore-based MEP Engineering Consultancy and Project Management company, providing professional services for commercial, industrial, institutional and residential developments.

Led by a highly experienced team with over 30 years of combined industry experience, we deliver end-to-end MEP solutions covering design coordination, BIM, construction management, authority submissions, testing & commissioning, and project handover.

We work closely with developers, consultants, and contractors to ensure projects are delivered on time, within budget, and in full regulatory compliance.

Vision

To be a trusted MEP engineering partner delivering compliant, efficient, and future-ready building solutions.

Mission

To provide high-quality MEP engineering, BIM, and project management services through strong technical leadership, regulatory compliance, and collaborative delivery.
