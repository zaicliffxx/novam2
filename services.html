What we do

Services

Construction redefined — end-to-end MEP consultancy from design through handover.

  • MEP Project Management & Coordination

    End-to-end management of Mechanical, Electrical and Plumbing works, ensuring seamless coordination between consultants, contractors and stakeholders for timely project delivery.

  • MEP Design Review, Value Engineering & Buildability Advisory

    Comprehensive design evaluation to enhance efficiency, reduce cost, improve constructability, and ensure compliance with project and regulatory requirements.

  • Authority Submissions & Regulatory Coordination

    Preparation and management of submissions to BCA, SCDF, PUB, SP Group, NEA, URA — ensuring full compliance with statutory regulations and approval processes.

  • Testing & Commissioning (T&C) Management

    Structured planning and supervision of testing and commissioning activities to verify system performance, reliability, and operational readiness.

  • TOP, CSC & Final Handover Documentation

    Coordination and preparation of documentation required for Temporary Occupation Permit (TOP), Certificate of Statutory Completion (CSC), and final project handover.

  • BIM Coordination, 3D MEP Modelling & Clash Detection

    Advanced BIM implementation including 3D modelling, interdisciplinary coordination, and clash detection to minimize site conflicts and rework.

  • Construction, Shop & As-Built Drawings

    Development and review of detailed construction drawings, shop drawings, and accurate as-built documentation reflecting final site conditions.

  • O&M Manuals and BIM Documentation

    Compilation of comprehensive Operation & Maintenance manuals and BIM documentation to support long-term facility management and asset lifecycle performance.
