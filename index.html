Who we are

Your trusted MEP engineering partner

NOVA M2 PTE LTD is a Singapore-based MEP Engineering Consultancy and Project Management company, providing professional services for commercial, industrial, institutional and residential developments.

Led by a highly experienced team with over 30 years of combined industry experience, we deliver end-to-end MEP solutions covering design coordination, BIM, construction management, authority submissions, testing & commissioning, and project handover.

Our services

End-to-end MEP engineering, BIM coordination, and project management for developments of every scale.

  • MEP Project Management & Coordination

    End-to-end management of Mechanical, Electrical and Plumbing works, ensuring seamless coordination between consultants, contractors and stakeholders.

  • Design Review, Value Engineering & Buildability

    View more

  • Authority Submissions & Regulatory Coordination

    View more

  • Testing & Commissioning Management
